# Corporate Company Profile Deck Prompt

Use this prompt to create a professional company profile presentation.

```text
@presentation-pro-designer

Create a professional corporate PowerPoint presentation.

Presentation type:
Corporate company profile.

Company / brand:
[Insert company name]

Purpose:
Create a clear, credible, and visually polished company profile deck that introduces the organisation, explains what it does, and presents its capabilities to stakeholders.

Audience:
[Clients / partners / investors / verification reviewers / internal leadership / general business audience]

Slide count:
[Insert number, or write: choose the best number based on content]

Use these sources:
- Use the attached company profile, verification pack, website content, brochure, or business document as the main source.
- Use the attached brand guide, logo, or PowerPoint template as the highest design priority.
- Do not use external sources unless I approve.
- Do not add unsupported claims.

Required structure:
- Title slide
- Executive overview
- Company identity
- Mission or positioning
- What the company does
- Core services or capabilities
- Markets or audience served
- Proof points, assets, platforms, or digital presence
- Team or ownership summary, if provided
- Contact or next steps slide

Content requirements:
- Keep the language factual, professional, and concise.
- Avoid hype, exaggeration, and generic marketing language.
- Use only claims supported by the attached files or user-provided information.
- If information is missing, mark it as "Not provided" or ask a clarifying question.
- Make the presentation suitable for business stakeholders.

Design requirements:
- Corporate, clean, modern, and brand-consistent.
- Follow the attached template, logo usage, colour palette, and typography if provided.
- Use visual cards, clean tables, icons, and section dividers.
- Use high-resolution visuals only.
- Avoid clutter, low contrast, and overdecorated slides.

Speaker notes:
Include concise speaker notes for each slide, written for a presenter introducing the company.

Clarification rule:
If the presentation type, audience, source authority, or brand style is unclear, ask up to 5 specific questions before creating the deck.

Output:
- Create a PPTX file.
- Include a short summary of sources used, assumptions made, design decisions, and QA checks completed.
```
