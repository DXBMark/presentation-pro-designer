# Visual Asset Quality

## Images

- Use high-resolution images suitable for slide display.
- Preserve aspect ratio.
- Crop intentionally.
- Avoid blurry screenshots, pixelated icons, and stretched photos.
- Use only user-provided or approved external images.

## Icons

- Prefer vector-style icons or clean monochrome/duotone icons.
- Keep stroke width and corner style consistent.
- Do not use emojis as structural icons in professional decks.

## Logos

- Use official logos only when provided or clearly available by user approval.
- Preserve proportions and clear space.
- Do not recolour, distort, crop, or add effects unless allowed by brand guidance.

## Visual QA

Check for overlap, low contrast, tiny labels, inconsistent icon scale, stretched assets, and off-brand decorative elements.
