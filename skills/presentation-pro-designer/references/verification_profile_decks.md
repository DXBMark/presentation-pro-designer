# Verification and Profile Claim Decks

Use for company profile claim reviews, domain ownership proof, profile correction, public listing verification, official channel confirmation, or entity identity support packs.

## Common Structure

1. Title and verification purpose
2. Entity overview
3. Company identity details
4. Authorized representative / ownership summary when supplied
5. Digital estate and domains
6. Official emails and communication channels
7. Public/social proof channels
8. Available verification methods
9. Clarifications, limitations, and not-a-certificate statements
10. Closing verification summary

## Critical Rules

- Use factual language.
- Do not imply legal incorporation, certification, or regulatory approval beyond the source.
- Include limitations exactly when the source provides them.
- Do not invent founders, ownership, domains, emails, or legal status.
- Use tables for identity, domains, and verification methods.
