# Business Proposal Decks

Use for client proposals, service offers, partnership proposals, implementation plans, and project proposals.

## Common Structure

1. Proposal title
2. Client context or opportunity
3. Problem or need
4. Proposed solution
5. Scope of work
6. Deliverables
7. Timeline and milestones
8. Team or capability fit
9. Pricing or options, only if supplied
10. Risks, assumptions, and dependencies
11. Next steps

## Rules

- Do not invent pricing, legal terms, warranties, guarantees, or delivery dates.
- Make assumptions explicit.
- Use a decision-ready structure.
- Include a clear next step.
