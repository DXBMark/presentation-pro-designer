# Investor Decks

Use for startup fundraising decks, investor updates, public-company investor presentations, equity-style slide reports, and investment committee decks.

## Startup Investor Deck

1. Title
2. Problem
3. Solution
4. Product
5. Market opportunity
6. Business model
7. Traction
8. Go-to-market
9. Competition
10. Team
11. Financials
12. Ask

## Public-Company / Equity-Style Deck

1. Executive snapshot
2. Investment thesis
3. Financial performance
4. Segment mix
5. Growth drivers
6. Peer comparison
7. Estimates and assumptions
8. Valuation
9. Risks
10. Appendix

## Rules

- Use `financial_support.md` for public-company or quantitative investor decks.
- Do not invent market size, traction, revenue, valuation, target price, upside, analyst rating, or multiples.
- Use `DATA NEEDED` for missing financial or market data.
- Clearly separate facts, assumptions, and opinions.
- Keep slides decision-ready, not report-heavy.
