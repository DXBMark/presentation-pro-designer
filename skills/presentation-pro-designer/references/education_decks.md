# Education, Teaching, and Learning Decks

Use for lessons, revision decks, training, workshops, CPD, student-facing materials, and educator resources.

## Required Decisions

- Audience age/year/level
- Lesson or session duration
- Learning objective or training outcome
- Source standard or syllabus
- Student-facing, teacher-facing, or blended
- Speaker notes and answers required or not

## Common Structure

1. Title / hook
2. Retrieval practice
3. Learning objectives and success criteria
4. Concept explanation
5. Worked example
6. Guided practice
7. Independent task
8. Misconception check
9. Assessment for learning
10. Exit ticket or reflection
11. Homework / extension if needed

## Quality Rules

- One learning job per slide.
- Use student-friendly language for student decks.
- Scaffold from simple to complex.
- Include checks for understanding where appropriate.
- Keep text large and readable.
- Add teacher notes with prompts, answers, timing, differentiation, and misconceptions when requested.
