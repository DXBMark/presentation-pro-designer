# Slide Strategy Patterns

Use a deck strategy that matches the user's goal.

## Education / Workshop

1. Hook or relevance
2. Outcomes
3. Prior knowledge
4. Core concept
5. Worked example
6. Guided practice
7. Independent application
8. Check understanding
9. Reflection or next steps

## Corporate Profile

1. Title
2. Executive overview
3. Who we are
4. What we do
5. Capabilities
6. Proof points
7. Operations or digital presence
8. Roadmap
9. Next steps

## Proposal

1. Context
2. Problem/opportunity
3. Proposed solution
4. Scope
5. Deliverables
6. Timeline
7. Investment or options, if supplied
8. Risks/dependencies
9. Why us
10. Next steps

## Investor Deck

Startup route: problem, solution, product, market, traction, business model, GTM, competition, team, financials, ask.

Public-company route: executive snapshot, thesis, performance, segment mix, peer comparison, estimates, valuation, risks, appendix.

## Verification Deck

1. Purpose
2. Entity overview
3. Identity details
4. Authorized representative
5. Digital estate
6. Official channels
7. Verification methods
8. Clarifications and limitations
9. Closing statement
